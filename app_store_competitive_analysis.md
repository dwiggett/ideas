# App Store Competitive Analysis: Contractor Bid Estimating

**Research Date:** July 7, 2026
**Platforms:** Apple App Store (iTunes API), Google Play Store (web scraping)

---

## Executive Summary

The app store landscape for contractor estimating reveals a **critical gap**: while there are many general-purpose invoicing/CRM apps and a few emerging AI estimating tools, there is **no dedicated, affordable, mobile-first bid creation tool** specifically for small/service contractors.

### Key Findings

1. **Top-rated apps are FSM platforms** (Jobber, Housecall Pro) — estimating is a side feature
2. **AI estimating is emerging fast** — SimplyWise (28K ratings), Handoff (1.7K ratings), Quotr Pro (30 ratings)
3. **No app combines**: quick bid creation + material cost database + trade-specific templates + AI assistance at $10-20/mo
4. **Most apps are free** with freemium models — the market expects free or very cheap
5. **ServiceTitan's mobile app is hated** (2.97/5 on iOS) — proving expensive enterprise tools have terrible mobile UX
