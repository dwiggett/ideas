# SoloBid — Non-Construction Service Markets
## Deep Dive Research Report
## Date: July 5, 2026
## Sources: HN Algolia, Reddit, G2, Capterra, App Store, Google Play

---

## Executive Summary

This report identifies non-construction service markets that need bidding/quoting software for solo operators and very small companies. The key finding: **every service market outside construction has the exact same problem — no affordable, simple, mobile-first quoting tool exists.**

The opportunity is massive: 20+ service markets, each with millions of solo operators, all using spreadsheets, Word docs, or nothing to create quotes and proposals.

---

## The Problem Across ALL Service Markets

### Who Has This Problem:
- 25 million solo service businesses in the US
- 50 million total small service businesses
- Every service provider who sends quotes or proposals

### The Pain (Universal Across All Services):
1. **Quoting takes too long** — 1-3 hours per quote
2. **Pricing is guesswork** — most don't know their true costs
3. **Tools are too expensive** — $49-299/month for what they need
4. **Tools are too complex** — built for larger companies
5. **No trade-specific templates** — every service is different
6. **No AI assistance** — no tool generates instant quotes
7. **Mobile-first is missing** — providers work from their phones
8. **Proposal generation is manual** — Word docs, Google Docs

1. **Jobber ($49-249/month)**
   - Strengths: All-in-one, scheduling, invoicing, CRM
   - Weaknesses: Too expensive for solo, complex, desktop-heavy
   - Target: 2-20 employee companies
   - Solo operator gap: YES — price point too high

2. **Housecall Pro ($49-199/month)**
   - Strengths: Good UX, scheduling, invoicing
   - Weaknesses: Expensive for solo, limited quoting
   - Target: 2-15 employee companies
   - Solo operator gap: YES — price and complexity

3. **ServiceTitan ($245-398/month)**
   - Strengths: Enterprise features, dispatching, reporting
